ABOUT COLLEGE


Brilliance Polytechnic,Bangalore, India is a premier educational institution of higher learning in the garden city of India, the Silicon Valley and the knowledge capital of India. The college is located in a calm, serene and eco-friendly environment in the back drop of the Hebbal lake surrounded by agricultural land and coconut groove of the University of Agricultural Sciences.

The college is recognized by Government, approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi which is the statutory body to approve professional courses and affiliated to Bengaluru University, the biggest in Asia having "five star" accreditation by NAAC. (National Accreditation and Assessment Council), instituted by Government.

With more than a decade of excellence in professional education and training. Brilliance Polytechnic is a leading college in Bengaluru. Over the years the Brilliance Polytechnic has established a growing legacy of innovative thinkers and exceptional standards of academic achievements and it is one of the regions top educational institution. Students from more than 22 overseas countries have been sponsored by Government of India through Indian Council for Cultural Relations (ICCR),New Delhi with scholarships to study various courses in our college.
